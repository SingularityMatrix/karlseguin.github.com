--- layout: quote title: "Innocence" theme: dark ---

They say:

Son, wait 'til you're older
for then you'll be wiser,
and these things you doubt
you'll figure out.

Age is no gift,
and experience no teacher.
Of course there's a shift
but it's made you no better.

The child is greater
than both mother and father.
All that they've gained
is a belief that they're smarter

They should be ashamed;
blind to such loss.
A wide chasm they've crossed
and innocence lost.
